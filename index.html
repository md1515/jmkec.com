Who we are

John Kelley Electric full service IBEW Local #134 electrical contractor offering all types of electrical services in residential, commercial, industrial, and institutional sectors throughout the Chicagoland area and beyond. We specialize in new construction and renovation projects as well as providing contract service maintenance for building owners and property managers. John Kelley can meet all WBE/MBE minority quotas. We have partnerships with several minority suppliers and subcontractors. In today’s competitive market, companies face many difficult decisions while attempting to maximize resources while minimizing operating costs. John Kelley Electric Corporation can help with your electrical needs by providing you with service, competitive pricing, and safety.
